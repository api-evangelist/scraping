The International Biosecurity Intelligence System, or IBIS, is a project that aims to detect and monitor emerging biosecurity issues.The results are primarily used by life scientists studying biosecurity for governments, but can also be used by farmers and other industry professionals to keep updated on biosecurity developments.The first phase of the system that focuses on aquatic animal health is already live, and you can check it out at: http://aquatic.animalhealth.org/.
