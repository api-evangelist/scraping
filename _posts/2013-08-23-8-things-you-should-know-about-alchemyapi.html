AlchemyAPI is the worlds most popular natural language processing service, allowing you to analyze text for keywords, entities, facts, sentiment, and more.If you are interested in getting started with AlchemyAPI, the following is a list of 9 things you should know: We provide 8 software development kits (SDKs) AlchemyAPI has software development kits (SDKs) available for 8 popular programming languages: Android OS, Java, Perl, Ruby, Python, PHP, C/C++ and C#.Use the SDKs to get AlchemyAPI quickly integrated into your application, or since AlchemyAPI is accessed through a simple REST interface feel free to write your own in your favorite programming language.All functions are accessible via REST interface AlchemyAPI utilizes a REST interface to access our text analysis algorithms.All endpoints are accessed through:  You can use either GET or POST requests for all functions, although it’s better to use POST if you’re sending a large amount of content in your request.
